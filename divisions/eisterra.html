Eisterra

Eisterra is believed, by many Terrans, to be their first home, and the first sign of life in their galaxy. Soon proved false by the Noids, with the discovery of other divisions, and ones within Eisterra itself.

However, Eisterra remains the highest forms of existence within the Cluster, and perhaps even the universe, with its ever-lasting food production, rich minerals, and intellectual beings.
