Lexicon of The Xynophod

Xynos (Xynos)

(zi-nose)

  • Xynos are the occupants of the Xynophod universe. A collection of diverse, idiosyncratic and gifted creatures, varying from one galaxy to another within the Arth Xynophod.

Eisterra (Eisterra)

(ice-tur-ra)

  • Our Earth, or Eisterra, inhabited by the Terrans, is one of the superior divisions of the Xynophod. Although all dimensions remain in one galaxy, Eisterra is the highest forms of existence.
