Xynophod is a universe of peculiar ways, and is a home of equally, slightly more, peculiar creatures. The inhabitants of the Xynophod vary from the creatures of the Cluster, which is a galaxy within the Xynophod, such as the Terrans and the Noids (who are exceptional beings that have paved the way for others to follow), to the ones who yet remain alien to the known Xynos. This universe gives way to the heritage of the Stones of Xynophod, which are distinct to every living entity contingent to the Divisions they belong to. These stones are the arbiter of live for these beings, given upon birth and activated upon pubescence, following a brutal fight against their most-alike from another division, for death or stone, resulting in only one survivor, as the other is subjected to a life-long ban from the universe if immortal. Thus, keeping the Xynophod a balanced home for the most deserving and potent.
